Fresh & Fusion — Drink Recipes

Target pour: 300ml per glass · No ice / ice cubes · Kitchen recipe card · Quantities per serve

No ice in any recipe. Chill milk, water, fruit, and juice in the cold bain marie before blending. Blend / juice first, then top up with a little milk or water if slightly under 300ml. Mark the serving glass at 300ml. Ginger shot stays a small shot (~60–80ml), not 300ml.

Kitchen tips (no ice)
  • Keep milk, coconut water, and cut fruit chilled in the cold bain marie (1–4°C) before prep.
  • Soft citrus / watermelon yield more juice; apple, carrot, beet need more raw weight.
  • If under 300ml: add 20–30ml chilled milk (shakes) or chilled water (juices). If over: cut liquid by 20ml next batch.
  • Weigh produce for cold-press; adjust next batch if yield ≠ 300ml.
