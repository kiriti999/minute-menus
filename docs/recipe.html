Kitchen tips
  • Scale under the bowl: add greens first, then toppings, stop at 300–350g.
  • Dressing cup is always 120ml — fill 110–120ml; lid on; go with salad.
  • Oats: if under 250g add milk; if over 280g reduce milk 15–20ml next batch.
  • Quinoa: 110–120g cooked ≈ 40g dry.
  • Wraps: warm tortilla lightly so it rolls without cracking.
